Add Gladiator Mode to the Writing Sprint app. This is a new 1v1 room mode called "gladiator" where two writers fight by writing. Here's exactly how it works:
Core mechanics:
Two writers enter the arena. Both start with 1000 HP
Writing heals you — every 10 words = +5 HP, capped at 1000
The word gap between the two writers constantly deals damage to whoever is behind
Gap damage scales with gap size:

Death gap selection: When creating a Gladiator room, the creator must choose the death gap — the word difference that triggers instant execution:
⚔️ 200 words — Brutal. Fastest and most unforgiving
⚔️ 400 words — Aggressive. Punishes slow patches hard
⚔️ 600 words — Standard. Balanced pressure throughout
⚔️ 800 words — Epic. Long, grinding, dramatic comebacks possible
The chosen death gap is displayed prominently in the room before the sprint starts so both writers know the stakes.
Combat mechanics:
Frenzy Heal — write 100 words in under 1:20 minutes, heal +50 HP instantly on top of regular healing
Wound — if your opponent has been ahead for 5 consecutive minutes without you closing the gap at all, your healing rate drops 30% until you get within 25% of the death gap
Last Stand — below 200 HP your healing rate doubles automatically
Momentum — close the gap consistently for 2 minutes straight and gain 20% bonus healing until your opponent pulls away again
Execution:
When the gap hits the chosen death threshold, the losing writer's screen shows a dramatic execution screen — dark, minimal, just the words "You have fallen" and their final stats
The sprint ends immediately, no waiting for the timer
Winner's screen shows "Victory" with their HP remaining and words written
If both survive the full sprint:
Whoever has more HP wins
If HP is within 50 points it's a Draw
Final screen dramatically reveals both HP values before declaring the winner
Crowd visual:
A crowd silhouette bar sits at the bottom of the screen, like a stadium crowd
The crowd is calm and still when the gap is in the safe zone
As the gap grows the crowd starts visually reacting — silhouettes raise arms, sway, become more agitated
When someone enters Last Stand the crowd erupts — arms up, chaotic movement, maximum energy
When execution happens the crowd freezes dramatically for 2 seconds then erupts
The crowd is purely visual, completely silent — no sound at all
No split screen. Each writer only sees their own writing area, their own HP bar, the gap meter, and the crowd. They do not see their opponent's text.
No announcer. No sound effects. No ambient noise. The experience is tense and silent except for the writer's own keyboard.
UI elements visible during the sprint:
Your HP bar at the top — thick, medieval style, draining in real time
Gap meter in the corner — showing current gap vs death gap as a filling bar, turns red as it approaches the threshold
Your word count and opponent's word count — numbers only, no text visible
Any active debuffs (Wound) or buffs (Momentum, Last Stand) shown as small icons
The crowd silhouette at the bottom reacting to the gap
Death gap threshold always visible as a red marker on the gap meter
Post match stats:
Closest gap moment — tightest the race ever got
Max gap reached — how close either writer came to execution
Total HP healed through writing
Number of times the lead changed hands
Time spent in the danger zone
Whether match ended by execution or timer
New WebSocket events needed:
gladiator_state — broadcasts both writers' HP, word counts, current gap, and active buffs/debuffs every second
gladiator_execution — fired when the death gap is reached, includes winner and loser IDs
gladiator_buff — fired when Frenzy, Momentum, Last Stand, or Wound activates or deactivates
Do not modify any existing modes. Gladiator mode is entirely additive. All existing behaviour stays exactly the same. Only two writers can be in a Gladiator room — if a third tries to join, show a message: "The arena is full. Two gladiators have already entered.
